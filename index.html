Good to know

Common questions

Is this really free?

Yes. It's a free educational workshop. If you'd like help afterward, you can book a complimentary consultation — but there's no pitch during the session.

I haven't opened probate yet. Too early?

It's actually the best time. Knowing the timeline before you start prevents most of the expensive mistakes we cover.

Do I need a lawyer first?

No. Most attendees come before hiring an attorney and leave knowing exactly what to ask one.

What if I can't make this session?

No problem — sessions run regularly and your Zoom link stays the same. Just join the next one that works for you.

Does this apply if my home isn't in San Diego County?

Yes. This workshop is built around California probate and trust law, so it applies anywhere in California — not just San Diego County. If your property is in another state, the process and timelines will differ.

Will my situation stay private?

Ask questions in chat without sharing details publicly. If you'd like to talk through your specific situation, you can follow up afterward by email.
